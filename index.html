I graduated from Indiana University in December 2017. The 3.5 years I spent living in Bloomington, Indiana while attending school were some of the best times of my life—which I’m often nostalgic for.

In Bloomington, just off of campus on a street called Kirkwood, there’s a bar called Kilroy’s—known for it’s $2 Tuesdays, "FAC" (Friday After Class patio party), and T-shirt Thursdays.

I loved T-shirt Thursdays (you pay a $5 cover for a surprise t-shirt) and this is my collection of Kilroy’s tanks, t-shirts, and sweatshirts from 2017. They usually spoofed other logos and designed the weekly t-shirt based on current school happenings (i.e. an IU Basketball player getting drafted to the NBA, etc).

This website was built with CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and some Photoshop. I used Draggabilly by David DeSandro for the drag-and-drop effect, and I learned the marquee effect in SuperHi's Advanced CSS + JS course.

You can find me on Twitter, GitHub, LinkedIn, and by email at madison.hardt@hey.com.
